About BCN

English-language comedy nights across Belgium.

Belgian Comedy Network produces stand-up shows for expats, internationals, students, date-night couples, and city-break travelers. The brand tone should feel sharp, warm, social, and confident — designed enough to feel real, but practical enough to update every week.

This concept leans into BCN’s actual operating model: a distributed promoter with recognizable city identities, recurring rooms, and fast outbound ticketing to Eventbrite.
