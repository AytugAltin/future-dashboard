    About BCN

    A network, not a single room.

    BCN produces English-language stand-up comedy nights across Belgium for expats, internationals, students, date-night pairs, and city-break visitors. The site needs to answer three things instantly: where the next room is, whether it feels credible, and where the ticket link lives.

    This concept leans into proof instead of polish-for-polish’s-sake: repeat venues, visible attendance signals, live Eventbrite connections, and city-by-city reassurance that BCN already has traction beyond a single Brussels room.

    It is intentionally honest where data is missing. Upcoming Ghent and Leuven public dates are not invented. Lineups stay labeled [PLACEHOLDER] until public data exists. The quote wall uses real BCN listing language rather than made-up audience reviews.
